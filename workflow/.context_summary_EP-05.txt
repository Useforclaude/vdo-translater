📚 MAIN TOPIC & TEACHING CONCEPT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**Core Concept**: "Reading Charts Like Reading a Story/Watching a Movie"

This is Part 3 of an advanced Price Action trading course. The instructor uses
an innovative metaphor system where:

- **Candlesticks = Characters** (แท่งเทียน = ตัวละคร)
  • Large candles = Main characters/protagonists (ตัวเอก, พระเอก)
  • Small candles = Supporting characters
  • Major candles = Commanders/Bosses (แม่ทัพ, บอส, หัวหน้า)

- **Price Movement = Story Plot** (การเคลื่อนไหวของราคา = โครงเรื่อง)
  • Uptrend = Hero/Bull side winning (พระเอก/กระทิง)
  • Downtrend = Villain/Bear side winning (ผู้ร้าย/หมี)
  • Consolidation = Evenly matched battle (สูสี)

- **Key Levels = Important Scenes** (แนวรับ/แนวต้าน = ฉากสำคัญ)
  • Support/Resistance = Critical plot points
  • Breakouts = Plot twists

**Teaching Goal**: Help traders understand price action intuitively by thinking
of charts as a story with characters, plot development, and scene analysis -
rather than just memorizing patterns and indicators.
